About the event

The Juju Charmer Summit is a special, free, three day event being hosted in Pasadena, California, USA. Enjoy three days of Juju sessions focused around OpenStack, NFV, Big Data, Benchmarking, and Container technologies like Swam, Kubernetes, and Mesos.

  • Three full days of charm training, talks, and workshops
  • Dedicated pair programming time with people from our ~charmers team to get your charms started, or finished, and into the charm store
  • Learn about the latest charm design techniques and tooling, as well as upcoming features in Juju itself
  • Dedicated free-flow, hands-on sessions to allow attendees to drive discussions/tutorials where they feel they need the most help
